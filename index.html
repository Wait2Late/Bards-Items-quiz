Instructions

You are to guess which three items Bard should prioritize for this generated matchup. You can also create your own matchups.

You can generate teams as many as you like, the suggested items will also reset.

You play Bard. Bard has many itemization choices at their disposal. Thus the logic and accuracy won't always be aligned with the player that may have a different opinion. The logic behind the choices are not perfect and perhaps never ever will choose supportive items.

This project started off by me watching Lathyrus's video about Bard's itemization and when to build them. It greatly inspired me to make this quiz, I really enjoyed his quiz segment and wanted more. I also wanted to create a new project to learn Typescript and make use of Leauge of Legends Api. What you see is a beginner's effort.
